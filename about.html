About Our Ministry

Founded 2020 by Apostle Daniel and Reverend Mrs Debby A Solomon

Our Vision

To raise true worshippers who worship God in spirit and in truth, transforming lives through the power of the Gospel and daily guidance of the Holy Spirit.

Our Mission

To spread the message of salvation through Jesus Christ, disciple believers in the faith, and provide daily spiritual nourishment through the "Sequence of God" devotional series.

Our History

True Worshippers Evangelical Ministries International was established in 2020 under the divine mandate given to Apostle Daniel Solomon and his wife, Reverend Mrs Debby A Solomon. The ministry emerged from a deep passion to see believers grow in their relationship with God through consistent daily devotion and worship in spirit and truth.

The name "True Worshippers" comes from John 4:23-24: "Yet a time is coming and has now come when the true worshippers will worship the Father in the Spirit and in truth, for they are the kind of worshippers the Father seeks. God is spirit, and his worshippers must worship in the Spirit and in truth."

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe in the divine inspiration and supreme authority of the Holy Scriptures as the Word of God.

Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, atoning death, bodily resurrection, ascension, and personal return.

Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit who indwells, empowers, and distributes gifts to every believer.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works.
